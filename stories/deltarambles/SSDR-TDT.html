# The Principles of Transitionary Socialism in Kultima

--- ### (meta) timeline: jmb | April 26, 2026 | 01:44 - 03:09 ### (meta) notes: --- ## Credits **Written by:** Princess Jomi I of Kultima, and Sir Mijo Cala of the Light **Published by:** The Royal Institute of Dajovy **Edited by:** Guardian Anastasia Rand, and Ms. Sera Sergeyevna **Translated by:** The Department of Language and Arts - R.I.D. **Available Official Translations in:** Dajovan (Original), Dajobskij, and Jodovski --- ## Background ### 'Old' and the Pre-Jomi Dajovy The pre-Jomi status quo, where a supreme council of capitalist owners (commonly known as the *Architects*) of production (and by extension, construction of the fortress city of Dajovy) elect a *'Supreme Guardian'* that is enforced by the **Silver Guards**. Currency was used in the form of silver coins known as 'Shield' which was used alongside the paper currency of the **credit system.** This credit system is quite similar to the current **'fiat'** system as to replace the old one, due to inability to fully replicate the *IPC's* method of **making** the credit. However, the only key difference is that the **Royal Bank**, controls the monetary policy more sustainably and accountable to the people. Credits and Shields are *deprecated* as currency, and *should* be traded to the bank in a 1:50 and 1:1 exchange rate. The current technology allows for the *new ruble* currency to be mass-produced using *dendro* to **produce** the **necessary** materials for the cotton-linen blend used. ### In Relation to Light World Literature ## Princples ### What is Socialism and Communism? For the first word, **Socialism,** it is defined as *'the transitional stage between capitalism and communism',* where: - The **means of production** are socially owned by the workers, that is *represented* by the state. - The distribution follows as, "From each according to their ability, to each according to their work". - Class distinctions dissolve, but some *old* elements of class, money and the state persist. In the transition to socialism, Jomi's ideas, which were supported by the people through the *rounds of voting* following each **important** change to the state, the stage of socialism followed by Kultima is for the **workers**, represented through the *'Royal Parliament of Kultima'* and the state's departments from it. Due to this transition, there are some class distinctions, such as the differences between overworld Dajovans and the rest of Kultima, including but not only; Pebble Town, and Novyjlevysk. The monetary system, as a symptom of taking over the old, remains as a contradictory force in the advancement of the people's material conditions, causing old relations to remain in some industries, due to the *hesitance* of *older* folks of their bureaucratic positions. For the second word, **Communism,** it is defined as *'the final stage of theoretical societal development'* where: - The **means of production** are commonly owned by the workers with *no distinctions* between the state and it's people. - The distribution follows as, "From each according to their ability, to each according to their needs". - A Classless, money-less and stateless organization of society can form. - True human freedom and equality is realized. This is still commonly debated as of today, and the princess, in the process of transitioning the state and the consideration of other states in dark worlds such as Scians-Chemistra, and any other *new* states from it, has let a *thousand flowers bloom* and ideas flourish in what this could look like. As of right now however, the princess and the people, through the debate and voting following the establishment of the *new parliament*, has agreed to keep the state, the princess and continue to analyze what a *future communist society* could look like. ---
